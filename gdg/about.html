About GDG on Campus

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the amazing community we're building at Sharda University.

Who We Are

Google Developer Groups (GDG) on Campus at Sharda University is a student-led community supported by Google Developers. We bring together passionate students who are excited about Google's technologies and eager to learn, share, and build innovative solutions.

Our chapter was established to create a vibrant ecosystem where students can enhance their technical skills, collaborate on projects, and prepare for successful careers in technology. We organize workshops, study jams, hackathons, and tech talks throughout the year.

Since 2020

Our Mission

To empower students with cutting-edge technology knowledge, hands-on experience, and industry connections. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment where everyone can explore, learn, and grow together.

Our Vision

To be the leading tech community in our campus, producing skilled developers who are ready to tackle real-world challenges and contribute to the global technology ecosystem.

What We Do

Workshops & Study Jams

Hands-on workshops covering Android, Web, Cloud, ML/AI, and more. Learn by doing with guided projects and expert mentorship.

Hackathons

Participate in exciting hackathons where you can build innovative solutions, compete with peers, and win amazing prizes.

Tech Talks

Listen to industry experts, Google Developer Experts, and successful developers share their experiences and insights.

Project Collaboration

Work on real-world projects with your peers, contribute to open source, and build your portfolio.

Networking Events

Connect with fellow developers, alumni, and industry professionals. Build relationships that last beyond college.

Certification Programs

Get certified in various Google technologies and boost your career prospects with industry-recognized credentials.
