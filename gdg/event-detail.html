About the Event

Join us for DevFest Sharda 2025, the biggest developer festival of the year! This full-day event brings together developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts from across the region for a day of learning, building, and networking.

DevFest is a flagship event organized by Google Developer Groups (GDGs) worldwide. Our edition will feature keynote speeches from industry leaders, hands-on workshops, technical sessions, and exciting opportunities to connect with fellow developers and Google Developer Experts.

What to Expect

  • Keynote Sessions

    Inspiring talks from industry leaders and Google Developer Experts

  • Technical Workshops

    Hands-on workshops on Android, Web, Cloud, AI/ML, and more

  • Networking

    Connect with developers, recruiters, and tech enthusiasts

  • Competitions & Prizes

    Participate in coding challenges and win amazing prizes

  • Swag & Goodies

    Exclusive GDG merchandise and sponsor goodies

  • Certificates

    Participation certificates for all attendees

Who Should Attend?

This event is perfect for students, professionals, and anyone interested in technology. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, you'll find sessions tailored to your level. Come with curiosity and leave with knowledge, connections, and inspiration!

Prerequisites

  • Bring your laptop for hands-on workshops
  • Basic programming knowledge recommended (but not required)
  • Enthusiasm to learn and connect with fellow developers
  • Valid student/professional ID for registration
